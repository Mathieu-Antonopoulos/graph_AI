Distribution des nœuds selon leur énergie spectrale ($v_2^2 + v_3^2$). À gauche : les ponts (ambigus). À droite : les cœurs de clusters (stables).

Spectral Embedding ($v_2, v_3$)

Nodes are mapped into the eigenspace where their position is defined by:
• Direction ($\theta$): The angle $\operatorname{atan2}(v_3, v_2)$ determines the cluster identity and the sweep order.
• Mass ($r$): The distance $\sqrt{v_2^2 + v_3^2}$ from the origin represents structural certainty. Core nodes are pushed outward, while bridges remain near the center.
